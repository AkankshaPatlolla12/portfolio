AI & ML Timeline


DESCRIPTION

Slide 1: How It All Began — key foundations (Turing’s test, Unimate, ELIZA) and the coinage of “artificial intelligence.”
Slide 2: AI Winters and the Rise of Deep Learning (1973–2023) — causes of winters (overpromising, compute limits, market collapse),
and the later resurgence through neural networks and modern tooling.
Each milestone includes a concise “why it mattered” to keep the story decision-useful for non-experts..

OBJECTIVE

Communicate the what/why of AI history so stakeholders can reason about today’s capabilities, recurring risks,
and why deep learning succeeded where earlier waves stalled.

PROCESS

Collected candidate milestones across academic, industry, and public-impact events.
Prioritized to a minimal narrative (foundations → winters → resurgence).
Wrote single-sentence “so-what” captions for each event.
Iterated layout for readability (decade markers, spacing, vertical guides).

TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES USED

Canva (layout and typography), lightweight style guide for consistent headings/captions.

VALUE PROPOSITION

Gives a fast model for AI’s cycles—helpful for scoping realistic projects, anticipating pitfalls (hype vs. delivery), and explaining why modern systems work.
